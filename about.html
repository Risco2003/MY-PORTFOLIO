About me

My name is Risco Lumamezi Mashete, aged 20. I am a beginner in front-end web development with skills in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I am currently a second year student studying for a Bachelor of Education in Secondary Education Honors Level 8 (English & Silozi) at the University of Namibia (UNAM).


I am passionate about education and technology, and I am always eager to learn more and grow in these fields. My goal is to combine my knowledge in education and my skills in web development to create educational tools that can help students learn more effectively.
